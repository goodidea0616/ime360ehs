BY: Alan Kruger B.Sc., MD



PROFESSIONAL QUALIFICATIONS

I am a duly qualified physician licensed to practice medicine in the provinces of Ontario and Nova Scotia (licence number 22748 Ontario and 007287 Nova Scotia) and am a member of the College of Family Practice of Canada and the American College of Occupational and Environmental Medicine. I obtained both a Bachelor of Science and Doctor of Medicine degrees from the University of Toronto. I have been on the Active Staff of William Osler, Etobicoke General Hospital as an Emergency Physician and in the Department of Family Practice. At Etobicoke General Hospital, I have held the positions of Acting Physician in Chief and President of the Professional Staff Association. I have served on the Medical Advisory Committee and the Board of Governors of the hospital. I continue to be involved in clinical medicine in Urgent Care and Aviation Medicine. I was the Senior Medical Consultant to L 3 Electronics of Canada (formerly Litton Systems) and am a Medical Consultant to Air Canada. I have also been appointed as a Civil Aviation Medical Examiner by Transport Canada. I have done Independent Medical Examinations for lawyers and for the WSIB as an NEL examiner.
